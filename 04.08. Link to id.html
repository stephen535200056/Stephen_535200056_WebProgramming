Arc Shot

A shot in which the subject is photographed by an encircling or moving camera

Interlude

A brief, interviewing film scene or sequence, not specifically tied to the plot, that appears within a film

Prologue

A speech, preface, introduction, or a brief scene preceding the main action or plot of a film; contrast to epilogue
